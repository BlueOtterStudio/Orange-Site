FAQ / Help

How do I reset my password?

Use the 'Forgot password' link on the login screen. If you still have trouble, contact support.

How do I request my data?

Request data export or deletion via Contact Support and specify your account email.

How long does support take to respond?

Typical response time is 1-3 business days. Provide as much detail as possible to speed up resolution.

Other help

For account or billing issues, include invoices, account email, and a short description when contacting support.
