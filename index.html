What Mandarin Orange does

All-in-one learning tools built to increase exposure, recall and speaking confidence.

  • Smart Spaced‑Repetition

    Adaptive flashcards that use proven spaced‑repetition algorithms to surface the right cards at the right time so you remember more with less effort.

  • Shadowing Practice

    Practice speaking by shadowing native audio. Record, compare and improve your pronunciation with real-time feedback and session history.

  • Bulk Card Creation & Auto‑Fill

    Create hundreds of flashcards in seconds using bulk import and smart auto‑fill features (auto-suggest pinyin, translations, and example sentences).

  • Built‑in Dictionary

    Look up characters, words and example sentences inline. Save dictionary entries directly to study cards or notes.

  • Notes for Study Sessions

    A simple notes app integrated with your study sessions so you can record insights, vocabulary and revision reminders tied to lessons.

  • Listening Practice & Tracking

    Build a personal media library (movies, shows, podcasts, videos) and track listening/exposure time to measure progress and retention.
