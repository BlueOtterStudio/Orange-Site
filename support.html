Support

Need help? Try these steps first:

  1. Check the FAQ.
  2. Search existing support emails or tickets (if you have access).
  3. Contact us at Aflatonlaw@gmail.com with details (device model, app version, steps to reproduce).

For urgent issues, include "URGENT" in the subject line. We aim to respond within 3 business days.

Self-help

Provide screenshots and exact text of any error messages. If a crash occurs, include the approximate time and what you were doing.
